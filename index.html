Winning Hands Explained
  • Royal Flush: 10 J Q K A of same suit
  • Straight Flush: Ordered rank sequence of same suit
  • Four of a Kind: 4 cards of same rank
  • Full House: 3 of a kind with a pair
  • Flush: Any 5 cards of same suit
  • Straight: Any ordered rank sequence
  • Three of a Kind: 3 cards of same rank
  • Two Pair: 2 pairs of cards of same rank
  • Jacks or Better: Any card pair of 10 J Q K A rank
